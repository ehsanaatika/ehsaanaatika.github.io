About

I am a psychodynamic psychotherapist dedicated to helping individuals explore the unconscious processes that influence their current behavior. Through a reflective and relational approach, I work with clients to uncover past experiences that shape present emotions and relationships, fostering self-awareness and emotional growth.

Services

  • Individual Psychodynamic Therapy
  • Online Therapy Sessions
  • Short-Term Focused Therapy
  • Therapeutic Support for Anxiety, Depression, and Trauma
  • Reflective Supervision for Practitioners
